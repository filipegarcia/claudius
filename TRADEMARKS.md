## How we reference Anthropic's marks

References to *Claude*, *Claude Code*, and the *Claude Agent SDK* in this
project's source, documentation, and marketing site are descriptive uses —
[nominative fair use](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nominative_use) — in the
same way an open-source project might say "for Node.js" or "works with
PostgreSQL." Specifically:

- Anthropic's word marks are used only to identify Anthropic's products
  factually (e.g. "Built with the Claude Agent SDK", "compatible with
  Claude Code").
- They are displayed no more prominently than necessary to communicate that
  factual relationship, and never more prominently than this project's own
  name.
- They are not styled, recolored, or combined to imitate Anthropic's own
  branding.
- No Anthropic logos, marks, or other visual brand assets are bundled in
  this repository.
